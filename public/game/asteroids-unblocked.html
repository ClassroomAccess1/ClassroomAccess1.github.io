Asteroids Unblocked – Classic Arcade Space Shooter

What Is Asteroids?

Asteroids sends you into a starfield filled with drifting rocks. Blast the large ones to split them into smaller pieces, avoid collisions, and survive as long as possible to stack high scores. Precision movement and quick shots are the keys to staying alive.

How to Play Asteroids Unblocked

  • Rotate: Left/Right Arrow keys spin your ship.
  • Thrust: Up Arrow accelerates in the facing direction.
  • Shoot: Press Space to fire bullets from your ship.
  • Break the Rocks: Large asteroids split into smaller ones—clear the field to advance.
  • Survive: Colliding with an asteroid costs a life; keep moving and shooting.

Tips & Tricks

  • Short Bursts of Thrust: Tap rather than hold to avoid drifting out of control.
  • Lead Your Shots: Fire where asteroids are heading, not where they are.
  • Stay Centered: More time to react versus hugging the screen edges.
