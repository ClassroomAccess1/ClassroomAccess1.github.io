Astray Unblocked – Dark Maze Puzzle Adventure

Astray is a 3D WebGL maze and puzzle game where you guide a glowing ball through eerie labyrinths with limited light. With Astray Unblocked, you can play directly in your browser—at school, work, or home—without downloads or restrictions.

What Is Astray?

Astray challenges you to survive in a series of dark, twisting mazes. Built with Three.js and Box2dWeb, the game combines smooth 3D graphics with realistic physics. Each movement feels heavy and deliberate as you roll your glowing ball through narrow corridors, dead ends, and hidden paths. The result is an atmospheric test of patience, memory, and precision.

Gameplay Features

  • Minimalist Controls: Use Arrow Keys (or H/J/K/L) to roll the ball in any direction.
  • Dark Maze Challenge: Limited vision forces you to rely on memory to avoid dead ends.
  • Progressive Difficulty: Levels grow bigger, trickier, and more disorienting as you advance.
  • Quick Guide: Hold the “I” key during play for controls and instructions.
  • Browser Ready: No downloads needed—play instantly with WebGL support.
