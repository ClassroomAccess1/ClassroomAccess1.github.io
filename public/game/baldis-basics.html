Baldi's Basics Survive the Schoolhouse

Baldi's Basics is a survival horror game with a satirical twist on 1990s edutainment titles. Players must collect seven notebooks while avoiding Baldi and a cast of quirky, unsettling school characters.

What is Baldi's Basics?

The game parodies old educational software while adding horror elements. Players navigate the school, solve math problems, and use items strategically to survive as Baldi becomes faster and more aggressive when mistakes are made.

How to Play Baldi's Basics?

  • Collect Notebooks: Find all seven notebooks scattered throughout the school.
  • Solve Math Problems: Correct answers help maintain safety; mistakes increase Baldi's speed.
  • Avoid Baldi: Navigate corridors and rooms carefully to escape the teachers pursuit.
  • Use Items: Grab items like soda, energy-flavored treats, and keys to aid your survival.
  • Escape the School: After collecting all notebooks, find the exit to win the game.
