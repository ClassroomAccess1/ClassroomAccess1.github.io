Bloons Tower Defense – Pop Every Balloon!

Bloons Tower Defense 1 is the original tower defense game by Ninja Kiwi, challenging players to stop waves of colorful balloons (bloons) from escaping. Place monkey towers strategically and upgrade them to survive 50 increasingly difficult waves.

What is Bloons Tower Defense?

The game tasks players with carefully positioning towers on a single track. You have five types of towers—Dart, Tack, Ice, Bomb, and Super Monkey—each with basic upgrades. With 40 starting lives, every missed bloon brings you closer to defeat.

How to Play Bloons Tower Defense?

  • Place Towers: Position monkeys to pop incoming bloons along the track.
  • Upgrade Towers: Improve damage, range, and special abilities.
  • Manage Lives: Start with 40 lives; prevent bloons from escaping to survive.
  • Complete Waves: Beat all 50 waves of increasing difficulty.
  • Strategize: Mix tower types and placements for maximum efficiency.
