What is Bomberman Tournament?

Bomberman Tournament is a 2001 action-adventure game for GBA that features two modes: a single-player Quest mode and a multiplayer Battle mode. In Quest mode, Bomberman explores dungeons, collects Carabon creatures, and rescues his friend Max. In Battle mode, up to four players can compete in classic Bomberman maze challenges using link cables.
