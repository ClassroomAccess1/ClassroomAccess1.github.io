Doodle Baseball Googles Fourth of July Classic

Doodle Baseball is an interactive baseball mini-game released by Google as part of the 2019 Fourth of July celebration. In this quirky game, players step up to the plate as food characters such as hot dogs, hamburgers, and soda cans, while the opposing team is made up of mischievous peanuts.

What is Doodle Baseball?

Doodle Baseball is a lighthearted sports game that celebrates Americas pastime with a playful twist. The objective is simple: hit pitches by clicking at the right moment and try to score as many runs as possible before striking out.

How to Play Doodle Baseball?

  • Wait for the peanut pitcher to throw the ball.
  • Click or tap at the right time to swing your bat.
  • Try to hit home runs for maximum points!
  • Keep batting until you get three outs.
