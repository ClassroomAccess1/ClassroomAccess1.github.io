Duck Life Train, Race, and Become a Champion!

Duck Life is an exciting online adventure racing game where you train a duck to compete in three disciplines: running, flying, and swimming. Level up your duck's skills and race to earn prize money to rebuild your farm.

What is Duck Life?

Duck Life is a fun and addictive duck racing game where you train your duck to win races. Originally built in Flash, it has now been converted to Unity WebGL, ensuring accessibility on modern web browsers.

Game Genre

Duck Life falls under multiple genres, including adventure, racing, and simulation. It combines skill-based training with competitive racing elements.

How to Play Duck Life

  • Train Your Duck: Improve its running, flying, and swimming skills through mini-games.
  • Compete in Races: Test your duck's abilities against others and win prizes.
  • Earn Coins: Collect coins to buy energy seeds, fancy hats, and new colors.
  • Rebuild Your Farm: Use winnings to restore the farm lost to a tornado.

Game Controls

Running

  • Click to jump

Flying

  • Click and drag to go up or down

Swimming

  • Up arrow to jump
  • Down arrow to dive
