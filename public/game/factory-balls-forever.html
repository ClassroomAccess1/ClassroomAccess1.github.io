Factory Balls Forever – A Creative Logic Puzzle by Bart Bonte

Factory Balls Forever challenges players to decorate balls so they perfectly match the design shown on the box. Using paint, stencils, masks, and tools, you’ll need to figure out the correct order of operations to solve each puzzle.

What is Factory Balls Forever?

Created by Bart Bonte, Factory Balls Forever is a modern revival of the original Flash puzzle series. The game focuses on logic, trial-and-error, and creative problem-solving without the pressure of time limits or penalties.

How to Play Factory Balls Forever?

  • Start with a Blank Ball: Each level begins with a plain white ball.
  • Match the Target: Look at the design on the box for the goal.
  • Use Tools & Paints: Apply stencils, dip the ball in paint, or remove layers using masks and pliers.
  • Experiment Freely: There are no penalties—mistakes are part of the puzzle-solving process.
