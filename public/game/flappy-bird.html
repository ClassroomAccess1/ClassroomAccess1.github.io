Flappy Bird – The Addictive One-Tap Arcade Challenge

Flappy Bird is one of the most iconic mobile games of all time. Players control Faby, a pixelated bird, and must guide it through narrow gaps in endless rows of pipes by tapping to flap its wings.

What is Flappy Bird?

Released in 2013 by developer Dong Nguyen, Flappy Bird quickly became famous for its brutal difficulty, retro 8-bit visuals, and highly addictive gameplay. It’s a game of precision timing where a single mistake ends your run.

How to Play Flappy Bird?

  • Tap to Flap: Click or tap to make Faby flap upwards.
  • Avoid Pipes: Guide the bird through the narrow gaps without touching anything.
  • Stay Balanced: Too many taps send you too high, too few drop you to the ground.
  • Score Points: Earn one point for every set of pipes you pass.
