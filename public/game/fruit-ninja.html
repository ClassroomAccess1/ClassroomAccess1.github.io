Fruit Ninja is a fast-paced arcade game where players swipe the screen to slice flying fruits, avoiding bombs, and aiming to create combos for higher scores.

What is Fruit Ninja?

Fruit Ninja challenges reflexes and precision. Players slice fruits that appear on the screen in different game modes, striving for combos and high scores while dodging bombs.

How to Play Fruit Ninja?

  • Swipe to Slice: Use mouse or touch controls to slice fruits on the screen.
  • Combo Slicing: Slice multiple fruits in one swipe for bonus points.
  • Avoid Bombs: Hitting a bomb ends the game or reduces lives.
  • Game Modes: Classic, Arcade, and Zen modes provide different challenges.
  • Unlockables: Collect coins to unlock blades, dojos, and power-ups.
