Geometry Dash Jump, Flip & Fly to the Beat

Geometry Dash is the legendary one-touch rhythm platformer released in 2013. With its deceptively simple controls, players guide a customizable icon through auto-scrolling levels packed with spikes, walls, and insane obstaclesall perfectly synced with the music.

What is Geometry Dash?

At its core, Geometry Dash is a rhythm-based action game. Players must jump, fly, and flip in time with the soundtrack to clear levels. Every beat matters, and every mistake sends you back to the startunless you practice strategically.

How to Play Geometry Dash?

  • Tap to Jump: One-button gameplaytap or click to leap over obstacles.
  • Switch Modes: Fly ships, roll balls, or wave through portals with unique physics.
  • Avoid Hazards: Spikes, walls, and traps end your run instantly.
  • Use Practice Mode: Place checkpoints to rehearse tricky sections.
  • Survive the Beat: Levels are synced with music, so rhythm is your key to survival.
