Gobble – Eat, Grow, and Outsmart the Puzzle

Gobble is a unique arcade-puzzle hybrid where you control a giant mouth emerging from the ground. The goal? Devour everything in sight—trees, boxes, rocks—to grow larger and solve environmental puzzles. The only catch: you must avoid eating people, or it’s game over!

What is Gobble?

Gobble flips the classic arcade style on its head by combining growth mechanics with puzzle-solving. Each level introduces new obstacles and layouts, requiring players to plan their “diet” carefully. Eat the right objects, grow big enough, and unlock the next stage.

How to Play Gobble?

  • Move & Eat: Guide the hungry mouth to devour rocks, trees, and boxes.
  • Avoid Humans: Eating people ends your run instantly.
  • Grow Bigger: The more you eat, the larger you become.
  • Solve Puzzles: Use your growing size to clear obstacles and reach new levels.
