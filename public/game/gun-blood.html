Gunblood – Western Quick Draw Duel Game

Gunblood is a classic browser-based quick-draw shooter where players face off in intense gun duels against AI opponents across nine rounds. Set in a Wild West theme, the game tests reflexes and precision as you aim and fire before your enemy does.

What is Gunblood?

Gunblood challenges you to duel various opponents in a test of speed and accuracy. Each duel begins with a countdown while your cursor must stay over your gun barrel. The moment the countdown ends, you must quickly draw and shoot your enemy before they shoot you. Bonus rounds between levels add extra challenge by requiring you to hit moving targets while avoiding civilians.

How to Play Gunblood?

  • Step 1: Place your mouse over the gun barrel during the countdown.
  • Step 2: As soon as the timer ends, aim and shoot your opponent as fast as possible.
  • Bonus Rounds: Test your accuracy by shooting bottles, birds, and other moving targets.
  • Objective: Survive nine rounds of duels to win the game.
