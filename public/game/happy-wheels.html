Happy Wheels – Survive the Crazy Obstacles

Happy Wheels is a side-scrolling, physics-driven game famous for its user-generated levels, unconventional vehicles, and dark humor. Players control ragdoll characters across insane obstacle courses, aiming to reach the finish line in one piece—or not!

What is Happy Wheels?

Happy Wheels challenges your timing, precision, and creativity. Each level is unpredictable, filled with traps, spikes, and hazards that often result in humorous, graphic consequences. Players can also create and share their own levels with the community.

How to Play Happy Wheels?

  • Choose Vehicles: Select from a variety of unconventional vehicles, like wheelchairs, bikes, and Segways.
