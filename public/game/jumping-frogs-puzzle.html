Jumping Frogs Puzzle A Classic Logic Brain Teaser

Jumping Frogs Puzzle is a timeless logic game where players must strategically move frogs across lily pads. The goal is simple but tricky: make the frogs switch sides by hopping over one another without breaking the rules. Known also as the Frog Hop Puzzle, this challenge has entertained puzzle lovers for decades.

What is Jumping Frogs Puzzle?

In this puzzle, frogs sit on lily pads in a line. Frogs of one color start on the left, frogs of another color on the right, and an empty space in the middle. By hopping and sliding into the gap, you must swap their positions perfectly. Each move counts, and only careful planning ensures success.

How to Play Jumping Frogs Puzzle?

  • Move Frogs: Frogs can move to an adjacent empty lily pad.
  • Jump Over: Frogs may jump over one opposing frog if theres an empty space behind it.
  • Swap Sides: The ultimate goal is to switch the two groups of frogs entirely.
  • Think Ahead: Planning is essentialrandom moves will leave frogs stuck.
