Papa's Burgeria – Grill, Build, and Serve

Papa's Burgeria is a classic Flipline Studios time-management game where you take on the role of Marty or Rita, working at Papa Louie’s famous burger shop. Your mission is to grill patties, stack toppings, and serve perfect burgers while keeping your customers happy.

What is Papa's Burgeria?

Papa's Burgeria challenges players to balance speed, precision, and customer satisfaction. As the orders pile up, you’ll need to manage grilling, assembling, and serving—all while earning tips to buy upgrades like timers, decorations, and cooking tools.

How to Play Papa's Burgeria?

  • Order Station: Take down customer orders carefully.
  • Grill Station: Cook patties evenly—don’t undercook or burn them!
  • Build Station: Stack buns, toppings, cheese, and sauces in the right order.
  • Earn Tips: The better your accuracy and timing, the bigger the tip.
  • Upgrade Shop: Buy timers, heat lamps, and décor to boost efficiency.
