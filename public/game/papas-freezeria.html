Papa's Freezeria – Run Your Own Ice Cream Shop

Papa's Freezeria is one of the most popular time-management simulation games from the famous Papa Louie series. Set on Calypso Island, you’ve landed what seems like the perfect summer job: managing a tropical ice cream shop. But when the tourist rush begins, you’ll need sharp multitasking skills to keep customers happy!

What is Papa's Freezeria?

Papa's Freezeria is a restaurant simulation game where you take customer orders and prepare frozen sundaes step by step. You’ll pour ice cream, add mixables, blend, and decorate sundaes with toppings to perfection, earning tips based on speed and accuracy.

How to Play Papa's Freezeria?

  • Take Orders: Greet customers and note down their sundae preferences.
  • Build & Mix: Pour ice cream, add mixables, and blend drinks at the Mix Station.
  • Top It Off: Add whipped cream, syrups, and toppings at the Topping Station.
  • Earn Tips: The faster and more accurate you are, the bigger your tips.
  • Upgrade Your Shop: Unlock new toppings, ingredients, and decorations as you level up.
