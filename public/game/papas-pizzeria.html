Your Journey to Becoming a Pizza Master: How to Play

Mastering Papa's Pizzeria requires focus across four distinct stations. Each plays a crucial role in delivering a five-star pizza:

  • Order Station: This is where it all begins! Listen closely to your customers' unique requests, noting down their preferred toppings, cheese, sauce, and crust types. Precision here prevents costly mistakes later on.
  • Topping Station: Grab the right dough and meticulously spread sauce and cheese. Then, carefully place each topping exactly where the customer wants it. Pay attention to their specific layout preferences – some like pepperonis spread evenly, others prefer them clustered!
  • Bake Station: Slide your topped pizza into the oven. This station demands keen observation. You'll need to bake the pizza until the crust is perfectly golden brown, indicated by the timer and visual cues. Over-baking or under-baking will lead to unhappy customers!
  • Cut Station: The final touch! Using the cutting guide, slice the pizza into the correct number of pieces (usually 4 or 8) and ensure each slice is uniform. An uneven cut can drastically reduce your customer’s satisfaction and your tip!
