Papa's Scooperia Bake, Scoop & Serve Sweet Treats

Papa's Scooperia is one of the most popular games in the Papa Louie series. Stranded in Oniontown without luggage, youre offered a job at Papas brand-new ice cream shop. Your task: bake cookies, scoop ice cream, add toppings, and serve delicious sundaes to customers while keeping up with demand.

What is Papa's Scooperia?

Papa's Scooperia is a time-management simulation game where you juggle multiple stationsmixing dough, baking cookies, scooping ice cream, and adding syrups, toppings, and extras. The better you balance speed and accuracy, the more tips and rewards youll earn.

How to Play Papa's Scooperia?

  • Take Orders: Customers will request specific cookie and ice cream sundae combinations.
  • Bake Cookies: Prepare cookie dough and bake it to perfection.
  • Scoop Ice Cream: Add ice cream scoops and toppings as ordered.
  • Serve Customers: Deliver the dessert exactly as requested to earn tips.
  • Upgrade & Unlock: Use tips to buy new ingredients, shop dcor, and upgrades.

Key Features

  • Cookie & Ice Cream Combo: Create custom sundaes with baked cookies and ice cream.
  • Multi-Station Gameplay: Balance baking, scooping, and topping stations.
  • Customer Variety: Serve quirky characters with unique sundae requests.
  • Upgrades & Rewards: Unlock new toppings, dcor, and gameplay features.
  • Mini-Games: Play fun side games between shifts to earn prizes.
