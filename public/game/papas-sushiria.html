Papa's Sushiria Roll Sushi & Serve Bubble Tea

Papa's Sushiria is a Flipline Studios time-management cooking game where you prepare sushi rolls and bubble tea for hungry customers. After accidentally breaking a lucky cat, you must work at Papa's Sushiria to pay off the debt.

What is Papa's Sushiria?

Papa's Sushiria challenges you to master sushi preparationcooking rice, rolling sushi, adding toppings, slicing rolls, and serving drinks. Precision and multitasking are key to keeping customers satisfied.

How to Play Papa's Sushiria?

  • Order Station: Take detailed sushi and bubble tea orders from customers.
  • Cook Station: Prepare sushi rice with perfect timing and accuracy.
  • Build Station: Assemble sushi with ingredients, toppings, and sauces.
  • Cut Station: Slice rolls evenly to please customers.
  • Tea Station: Craft bubble teas with flavor, milk, and tapioca pearls.
  • Serve & Earn: Deliver sushi and drinks quickly to earn tips and unlock new content.
