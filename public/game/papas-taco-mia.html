Papa's Taco Mia Grill, Build & Serve Tasty Tacos

Papa's Taco Mia puts you in charge of a taco shop where you prepare tacos with grilled meats, toppings, and sides to satisfy hungry customers.

What is Papa's Taco Mia?

Papa's Taco Mia is part of Flipline Studios Papa Louie series. It challenges you to run a taco stand, cooking meats, adding toppings, and balancing sides to please customers and earn tips.

How to Play Papa's Taco Mia?

  • Take Orders: Customers order tacos with specific meats, toppings, and sides.
  • Grill Station: Cook meats to perfection without burning them.
  • Build Station: Assemble tacos with requested ingredients, toppings, and sauces.
  • Chip Station: Serve tacos with chips and salsa for the full meal.
  • Serve & Earn: Deliver tacos quickly and accurately to earn points and tips.

Key Features

  • Upgrades & Unlocks: Earn tips to improve your shop, unlock new ingredients, and customize the taco stand.
  • Customer Challenges: Keep picky Closers and food critic Jojo satisfied.
