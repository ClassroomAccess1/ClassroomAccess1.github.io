Play R.E.P.O – Online Co-op Horror Extraction Game

R.E.P.O – Unblocked is a tense, cooperative horror experience where up to six players take on the role of robotic workers collecting valuable salvage for their employer, the Taxman. Combining teamwork, physics-based gameplay, and terrifying encounters, R.E.P.O challenges you to extract high-value items without getting destroyed by lurking horrors.

What is R.E.P.O?

R.E.P.O stands for “Recovery Extraction Protocol Operators.” Players are tasked with entering abandoned industrial zones, derelict facilities, and other hazardous locations to collect and safely extract valuable items. Every mission is different thanks to randomized layouts and unpredictable threats. Communication and precision are key to survival.

How to Play R.E.P.O

  • Work Together: Join up to five other players to recover and transport fragile or heavy items.
  • Use Proximity Chat: Communicate with your teammates through in-game voice chat for realistic coordination.
  • Avoid Monsters: Stay quiet and use strategy to evade terrifying creatures that stalk the area.
  • Manage Physics: Handle objects carefully—dropping or damaging them lowers their value.
  • Extract Safely: Deliver the items to extraction points to earn SURPLUS currency for upgrades.

Why Play R.E.P.O?

R.E.P.O blends the intensity of horror survival with the teamwork of co-op extraction games like Lethal Company or Phasmophobia. Every session feels unique, with randomized maps, unpredictable enemies, and tense moments that test your teamwork and composure.

Key Features

  • Co-op Gameplay: Play with up to six friends using proximity chat.
  • Physics-Based Items: Carefully carry and transport objects without damaging them.
  • Roguelike Elements: Randomized missions and environments ensure replayability.
  • Upgrade System: Use SURPLUS currency to buy weapons, tools, and permanent upgrades.
  • Immersive Horror: Face eerie sound design, dynamic lighting, and unpredictable threats.

Enter the Extraction Zone

Gather your crew and step into the unsettling world of R.E.P.O. Can you recover the goods, survive the monsters, and make it out alive? Your employer is waiting… and the Taxman always collects.
