Tactical Assassin 2 – Stickman Sniper Game

What is Tactical Assassin 2?

The game follows a skilled stick-figure assassin who takes on dangerous contracts. Each mission requires careful observation, identification of the correct target, and flawless execution. Killing the wrong person or harming bystanders results in failure, making planning and accuracy just as important as shooting skill.

How to Play Tactical Assassin 2?

  • Step 1: Read the mission briefing to understand your objective.

  • Step 3: Avoid shooting innocents – precision is critical.
